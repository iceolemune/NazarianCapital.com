ABOUT PAGE
Page Headline

An Operator's Perspective on Capital

Firm Story

Nazarian Capital was founded by Benjamin Nazarian on a straightforward observation: most companies that struggle to scale don't have a capital problem — they have an operational one. They've never designed systems that survive five times the headcount. They've never navigated the complexity of expanding a DTC business into retail across domestic and international markets. That's the gap Nazarian Capital was built to fill.

The firm draws on deep operational experience built across real estate, technology, and private enterprise — including founding and scaling Omninet Capital, a multifamily real estate platform, and serving as an active strategic partner to portfolio companies through roles at Parkview Financial and Constellation Capital. These aren't advisory relationships. They are working partnerships where the firm sits alongside the CEO, helping them build the infrastructure, teams, and systems that sustain long-term growth.

Nazarian Capital also brings deep expertise and an extensive network in the health and wellness industry, with a dedicated focus on identifying and investing in the next generation of consumer health and wellness companies.

Philosophy Principles

Operational experience is the scarcest resource.

Most founders are building their first company. The challenges they face — scaling teams, designing processes, managing culture under pressure — are ones that only experience can navigate well.

Patient capital outperforms impatient capital.

Nazarian Capital takes long-term positions. The goal is never a quick exit. It's building something that lasts.

Resources matter as much as money.

Portfolio companies gain access to an operational network and a founder who has been through the growth curve — and come out the other side.

Benjamin Nazarian — Bio

Benjamin Nazarian is a seasoned operator and investor with decades of experience founding, scaling, and advising companies across real estate, technology, and consumer markets. He is the founder of Omninet Capital, a multifamily real estate platform, and serves as an active strategic partner to growth-stage companies through his roles at Parkview Financial and Constellation Capital. Benjamin brings hands-on operational expertise in business planning, DTC-to-retail expansion, marketing, and organizational scaling — the exact challenges most founders face for the first time. Nazarian Capital is the expression of that experience, deployed as capital.

Sienna Nazarian — Bio

Sienna focuses on identifying early-stage consumer product companies in the health and wellness space. With a background in product design engineering, she brings a founder-level lens to evaluating how products are built, differentiated, and positioned for scale. Her coverage expands Nazarian Capital's reach into one of the fastest-growing segments of the consumer market.

STRATEGY PAGE
Page Headline

Two Ways to Invest. One Underlying Principle.

Intro

Nazarian Capital takes a deliberate, two-track approach to investing — each driven by a different kind of conviction, and each designed to generate returns over a long time horizon.

Track 01 — Operator Partnerships

For Companies That Need More Than a Check

These are investments in venture and growth-stage companies where capital alone isn't enough. Nazarian Capital takes an active role — working directly with the CEO to build the operational foundations that early-stage companies typically lack.

What this looks like in practice:

  • Working with the founder to develop the business plan and establish a clear path to scale
  • Developing cost-effective marketing plans with a disciplined focus on ROI
  • Guiding expansion from DTC into retail, across both domestic and international markets
  • Implementing operating systems, reporting, and accountability frameworks
  • Serving as a sounding board on strategy, culture, and the decisions that don't have obvious answers
Track 02 — Growth Capital

For Companies That Have Already Proven It

Selective, high-conviction positions in established companies with demonstrated market leadership and meaningful paths to continued expansion. This is passive, long-term capital placed with conviction.

What we look for:

  • Category leadership in a large or expanding market, with emphasis on AI, defense tech, and fintech
  • A capital structure that respects existing investors
  • Management teams with a track record of execution
  • A long runway for continued value creation
Underlying Principle

"Is this a business worth owning for a decade? Nazarian Capital does not chase trends. It backs passionate founders."
